Ben Clary's Photo Page
This website was created to showcase my skills in coding, as well as to show some samples of my photography. In my photos, I try to focus mainly on showing human beings interacting with harsh, gigantic, or otherwise dramatic landscapes. I feel that this gives a sense of insignificance to the human subjects, while simultaneously making them the true focal points of each image.
